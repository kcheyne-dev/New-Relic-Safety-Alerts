What's different from before

  • One header button instead of two — 🗺 Map Tools with a green count badge showing how many overlays are toggled on. Reduces header clutter; surfaces "is anything on?" at a glance.
  • Tabbed dropdown — Layers & Filters / Geo-fence. Same content as before, organized cleaner. The fence-active dot now lives on the Geo-fence tab.
  • Same trigger pattern — click button to open, click outside or hit Esc to close. Clicking the button while open closes it.
  • Tab state is transient — not persisted. Always boots to Layers tab. (We can persist if you want it to remember last tab.)
  • No fence-state lost — drawing a fence in the Geo-fence tab works exactly as before. Switching tabs while a fence is drawn keeps the fence on the map.
  • Esc precedence: modal → tools dropdown → reset map view. Same chain as before, simpler now (one less dropdown to check).
